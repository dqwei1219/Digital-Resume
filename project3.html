Community-Based Secure Micromobility Storage with SANDAG

  • Showcase Presentation Link
  • Detailed Documentation Link

    Description:

  • Context: Use of personally-owned micromobility is on the rise yet secure public storage options are severely lacking.
  • Issue: This deters people from using micromobility due to high risk of theft and vandalism.
  • Solution: Create a feasible solution that benefits the San Diego community by pairing convenient and secure micromobility parking solutions with bikeways in neighborhoods people live, work, and play.

Project Accomplishment:

  • Identify feasible types of micromobility storage (lockers, group facilities, on-street, etc.)
  • Create a feasible micromobility storage that benefits the San Diego community.
  • Estimate a Rough order of magnitude costs (right-of-way permits, physical infrastructure, maintenance, security/enforcement, supporting outreach
